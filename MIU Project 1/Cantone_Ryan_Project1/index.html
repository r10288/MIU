Weapon Selection

  • Weapon Description

    Handgun

    A handgun is any firearm that can be held and fired with one hand.

    SMG

    A submachine gun (SMG) is an automatic carbine, used at close to medium range.

    Shotgun

    Shotgun firearm that is usually designed to be fired from the shoulder. Very powerful at close range!

    Rifle

    A rifle is a firearm designed to be fired from the shoulder, used for medium to long range very affective all around weapon.

    Bow

    A bow is an archery weapon that uses elasticity to propel arrows

    Sword

    A sword is a bladed weapon used primarily for cutting or thrusting.

    Axe

    The axe usually one sided sometimes double sided used to cut wood or other objects, and with it's bladed edge used as a weapon.

    Dagger

    A dagger a very short bladed weapon, can be concealed used at close range a prefered weapon of assassins and thieves..

    Mace

    A mace is a weapon with a heavy head on a solid shaft used to crush opponents.

    Explosive

    An explosive used to make an explosion of fire and shrapnel to hurt and or injur an entire group of enemies.

    Other

    Any other item that can be used with the aim of causing damage or harm to anything in the environment.
